Speed, Strategy and Legacy

Since its origins in the mid-20th century, Formula One (F1) has stood as the pinnacle of single-seater auto racing. Each season, teams and drivers race across global circuits, vying for the esteemed World Championship titles. From its early days of raw speed to today's complex blend of technology and strategy, F1 has continually evolved.

This website provides an in-depth look at F1 from 1950 to 2023, using data sourced from Formula 1 World Championship 1950-2020 dataset on Kaggle. Dive in as we discover which drivers succeed based on the constructor team they compete for and the evolution of F1 across the years.
